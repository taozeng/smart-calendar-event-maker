Smart Calendar Event Maker

How to use:

1. Highlight the content with the location and time information.
2. Right click and select "Create new event".
3. Modify the data fields if necessary.
4. Click "Confirm" and save it in Google Calendar.

- Recognize the US/Canadian address.
- Recognize the date and time (such as noon, tomorrow or 8:30am).
- Recognize common venues (such as Husky Stadium).
- Recognize common holidays (such as the Mother's Day).
- Support time-zone.
- Use Option to choose treating 06/04 as June 4 (default) or 6th April.
